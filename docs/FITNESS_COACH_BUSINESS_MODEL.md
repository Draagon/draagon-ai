## The New Tier: Asynchronous Video Review

### The Gap in the Market

| Tier | Price | What You Get | Problem |
|------|-------|--------------|---------|
| **AI Only** | $15-35/mo | Algorithm feedback | No human touch |
| **??? GAP ???** | $49-79/mo | ??? | Nothing exists here |
| **Live Coaching** | $150-200/mo | Real-time calls | Expensive, scheduling hard |

**The solution: Asynchronous Video Form Review**

Users record their movements → Mike (or trained reviewers) watch and provide audio/video feedback → User gets personalized correction without scheduling a live call.

### How It Works

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                     ASYNC VIDEO REVIEW FLOW                              │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  1. USER RECORDS                                                         │
│     • Opens app, records movement (30-60 sec)                           │
│     • AI does initial form check (basic feedback)                       │
│     • Video queued for human review                                     │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  2. MIKE/REVIEWER WATCHES                                               │
│     • Dashboard shows pending reviews                                   │
│     • Reviewer watches, records audio commentary                        │
│     • Can draw on video (like sports coaching apps)                     │
│     • ~3-5 min per review                                               │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  3. USER RECEIVES FEEDBACK                                              │
│     • Push notification: "Mike reviewed your movement!"                 │
│     • Watches video with audio overlay                                  │
│     • Sees annotated frames with corrections                            │
│     • Can ask follow-up questions (async)                               │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Why This Works

1. **Time zone friendly** - Perfect for Asia (Mike reviews at his convenience)
2. **Scalable** - Mike can review 20+ videos/day vs 4-5 live calls
3. **Higher quality feedback** - Mike can pause, rewind, annotate
4. **Lower price point** - $49-79/mo vs $199 for live coaching
5. **Users can re-watch** - Feedback is persistent, not ephemeral

### Competitor Analysis: Video Form Check

| Platform | Model | Pricing | Notes |
|----------|-------|---------|-------|
| [Onform](https://onform.com/) | Video analysis tools | $29-79/mo | Tools for coaches, not consumer app |
| [Gymscore](https://www.gymscore.ai/) | AI form analysis | Freemium | AI-only, no human review |
| [MovementLink](https://movementlink.fit/) | Human form check | $195/mo | Expensive, uses Coach's Eye |
| [Yogger](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/yogger-movement-analysis-app/id1576592816) | Movement analysis | Varies | Motion capture focused |

**Gap:** No one offers AI + Human async review at an accessible price point ($49-79).

### Mike's Review Capacity

| Scenario | Videos/Day | Time/Day | Monthly Capacity |
|----------|------------|----------|------------------|
| **Solo Mike** | 20 | 1.5 hours | 600 reviews |
| **Mike + 1 reviewer** | 50 | 3.5 hours total | 1,500 reviews |
| **Mike + 3 reviewers** | 100 | 7 hours total | 3,000 reviews |

At $59/mo with 2 video reviews/month per user:
- 600 reviews = 300 users = **$17,700/mo** from this tier alone

### Implementation with draagon-ai

The AI component adds value even before human review:

1. **Initial AI Analysis** - Basic form feedback (posture, alignment, common errors)
2. **Smart Queuing** - Prioritize reviews by subscription tier, urgency
3. **Memory** - AI remembers past feedback, notices improvement/regression
4. **Follow-up Questions** - AI handles routine follow-ups, escalates complex ones
